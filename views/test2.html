LEARN TO RIDE

At Moto-U, we educate prospective and returning motorcyclists so they can make the right choices, choices which may lead them to one day becoming safe and responsible riders.

These choices may also lead them to not riding on the street, and we think that's okay, too.

We've determined that the best way to train new riders is to be sure their learning time is safe, fun, enjoyable, affordable, comfortable, and most importantly, not rushed. That's why we spend twice as long teaching basic motorcycle operation as many other schools. That lets you learn at your own pace in a safe, controlled environment. These classes cover your skills set – how to start, stop, turn, and operate a basic, small motorcycle.

BEGINNER MODULE: Classes 101 - 103 are three, three-hour sessions using our 125cc (beginner) motorcycles, helmets, and gloves.
If you're struggling and need more practice, you can take the class again at no charge if the instructor recommends it. -That means you only pay for the portion of training you can use!

EXPERIENCED BEGINNER MODULE: Class 104 is a 3-hour overview session that combines all skills taught in 101-103 and introduces skills needed for riding on the street.
Use our motorcycle, helmet, and gloves on a closed, off-street range.
